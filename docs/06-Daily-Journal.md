This journal serves as a record of my AI Engineering journey.

Rather than simply tracking what I studied, it captures:

- Daily objectives
- Work completed
- Technical challenges
- Solutions implemented
- Key learnings

- Interview preparation
- Next steps

The goal is continuous improvement through reflection and documentation.
