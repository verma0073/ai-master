# Overview

The AI Master project uses a modern, production-oriented technology stack that reflects current industry practices in AI Engineering, Generative AI, MLOps, and AI Platform Engineering.

Technologies are selected based on:

- Industry adoption
- Production readiness
- Community support
- Scalability
- Career relevance
- Interview importance

# Engineering Principles

Every technology included in this stack must satisfy at least one of the following:

- Used in production AI systems
- Relevant to AI Engineering roles
- Frequently discussed in interviews
- Enables scalable AI application development
- Supports cloud-native deployment
- Enhances automation or observability

Technologies that do not align with these principles should only be introduced when they provide clear value to a project.
